# Copywriting Rubric

Each slideshow is scored out of 100.

| Criterion | Points | Meaning |
|---|---:|---|
| Hook specificity | 20 | Names a private, recognizable behavior |
| Retention chain | 15 | Each slide creates a reason to continue |
| Emotional resonance | 15 | Feels true without melodrama |
| Novel reframe | 15 | Avoids generic advice |
| Cherry fit | 10 | Naturally leads to small social reps/logging |
| Safety/compliance | 10 | No cure/treatment/clinical claims |
| Engagement trigger | 10 | Clear comment/save/share prompt |
| Visual evocativeness | 5 | Symbolic background prompt has mood/progression |

Hard fail:

- claims Cherry treats/cures anxiety
- tells users to stop medication/therapy
- shame-based copy
- literal trauma imagery
- no app/CTA path
